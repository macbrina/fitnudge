## Design Guidelines

### Brand Colors

```css
Primary Blue: #2563EB
Dark Blue: #1E40AF
White: #FFFFFF
Dark Gray: #1F2937
Light Gray: #F3F4F6
```

### Typography

- **Headlines:** Bold, impactful (Space Grotesk or similar)
- **Body:** Clean, readable (Nunito or similar)
- **Accents:** Use brand blue for highlights

### Visual Style

#### Do's ‚úÖ

- Clean, minimalist layouts
- Consistent brand colors
- High-contrast text for readability
- Authentic, real photos when possible
- Subtle animations and transitions
- White space is your friend

#### Don'ts ‚ùå

- Cluttered designs
- Too many fonts
- Low-quality images
- Inconsistent branding
- Hard-to-read text on busy backgrounds

### Templates to Create

1. **Quote Template** - Brand colors, clean typography
2. **Carousel Template** - Consistent header/footer, slide numbers
3. **Story Template** - Poll backgrounds, question prompts
4. **Announcement Template** - Bold headline, CTA
5. **Testimonial Template** - Photo + quote + name
6. **Feature Showcase Template** - App screenshot + description
7. **Before/After Template** - Split design comparison

### Tools for Design

- **Canva** - Quick social graphics
- **Figma** - Complex designs, templates
- **CapCut** - Video editing for TikTok/Reels
- **Lottie** - Animations

## Scaling Tactics

### Phase 1: Organic Growth (0-10K followers)

1. **Consistency is King**
   - Post daily across all platforms
   - Engage with every comment
   - Respond to DMs within 24 hours

2. **Trend Jacking**
   - Jump on trending audios within 24-48 hours
   - Adapt viral formats to your niche
   - Use trending hashtags appropriately

3. **Community Building**
   - Engage with similar accounts
   - Comment on influencer posts (add value, don't spam)
   - Join relevant conversations

4. **Cross-Promotion**
   - Repurpose content across platforms
   - Link between platforms in bios
   - Tease platform-exclusive content

### Phase 2: Accelerated Growth (10K-100K)

1. **Influencer Partnerships**
   - Micro-influencers (10K-50K): Product gifting
   - Mid-tier (50K-200K): Paid partnerships
   - Fitness/productivity niche focus

2. **User-Generated Content (UGC)**
   - Create branded hashtag challenges
   - Feature user content weekly
   - Incentivize testimonials

3. **Paid Advertising**
   - Start with $500-1000/month
   - Test multiple creatives
   - Focus on app install campaigns

4. **Collaborations**
   - Partner with complementary apps
   - Guest appearances on podcasts
   - Co-created content series

### Phase 3: Scaling (100K+)

1. **Content Team Expansion**
   - Hire content creators
   - Invest in higher production quality
   - Create platform-specific content

2. **Ambassador Program**
   - Recruit power users as ambassadors
   - Provide exclusive perks and early access
   - Create ambassador-only content

3. **Paid Partnerships at Scale**
   - Macro-influencers (200K+)
   - Celebrity partnerships
   - Brand collaborations

4. **Multi-Language Content**
   - Create content in Spanish, French, German
   - Partner with international creators
   - Localize campaigns for different markets

## Notes

- **Authenticity wins.** People connect with real stories, not perfect marketing.
- **Consistency beats perfection.** Post regularly, even if it's not perfect.
- **Engage, don't broadcast.** Social media is a conversation, not a megaphone.
- **Test and iterate.** What works today might not work tomorrow.
- **Celebrate milestones.** Share your journey ‚Äî people love rooting for underdogs.
